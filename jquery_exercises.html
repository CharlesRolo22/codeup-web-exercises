Fruit Smootie Ingredients

  • 1 cup of strawberries
  • 1 cup of pineapples
  • 1 cup mixed berries
  • 1 banana
  • 1.5 cup of almond milk
  • 1/4 cup of almond butter
  • 1/4 cup peanut butter

Mix fruit in blender, add almond milk and additive to mixture, mix until smooth pour into container and enjoy.
